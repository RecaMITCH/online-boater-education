About Us

Making boater safety education accessible, engaging, and easy for everyone.

Our Mission

OnlineBoaterEducation.com was built with a simple belief: every boater deserves easy access to quality safety education. Boating is one of the most popular recreational activities in America, and we think the path to safe, legal boating shouldn't be complicated.

We partner with Recademics to deliver state-approved boater education courses that meet NASBLA standards. Whether you're a first-time boater or need to renew your certification, our platform makes the process seamless.

Our courses are designed to be completed at your own pace, on any device, from anywhere. No rigid schedules, no cramped classrooms — just clear, engaging content that prepares you to be safe on the water.
